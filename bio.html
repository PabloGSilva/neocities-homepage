Cool slogans... but who actually are you?

Well, my name is Pablo A. G. Silva Jr., and i am a year old latino chico from São Paulo - Brazil. That only really on paper, really, since i moved to the city of Garça when i was about 4 years old. Well, i currently work as a software developer and I have a degree in systems development from the FATEC Technology College in Garça.

I have developed a passion for music over the years, which sparkled off my step-father introducing me to Pink Floyd's "Comfortably Numb". Safe to say that my mind was blown at that moment, and music never felt more intense before. Eventually i got into all the cool classic rock bands, y'know, The Beatles, The Doors, Zeppelin, etc. Since then my musical taste has morphed into a hybrid between the frantic extreme punk of Orchid's "I Am Nietzsche" to the beautiful aquatic soundscapes of Hiroshi Yoshimura's "Wet Land", with some Chappell Roan thrown in the rotation too. So yeah i am an autistic little brat about anything music, even the least musical sounding records you can find.

I'm also an enthusiast of topics like tech development, history, politics, cinema, gaming...

Trust me, when the socialist revolution starts i'll be there on the front lines hehehe.
